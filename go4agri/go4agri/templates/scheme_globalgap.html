GLOBALG.A.P. Certification

Certification Services (Under Development)

GO4AGRI Certification Pvt. Ltd. is in the process of expanding its certification scope to include GLOBALG.A.P. certification services, one of the world’s leading standards for Good Agricultural Practices (G.A.P.).

GLOBALG.A.P. certification provides assurance that agricultural products are produced in compliance with internationally recognized requirements for food safety, traceability, environmental sustainability, and worker welfare.

Applicable Standards

  • GLOBALG.A.P. IFA Version 6 (SMART / GFS)
  • Control Points and Compliance Criteria (CPCC)
  • GLOBALG.A.P. General Regulations

Proposed Scope

  • Producer (Option 1)
  • Producer Groups (Option 2)

Add-ons

  • GRASP – Social compliance and worker welfare assessment
  • SPRING – Sustainable water use and irrigation practices

Certification Approach

Certification will be conducted through application review, inspection, certification decision, and surveillance in accordance with ISO/IEC 17065 principles.
