Register a Complaint or Appeal a Certification Decision

Go4Agri is committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity. If you have concerns regarding certification decisions, misrepresentation of organic status, or staff conduct, please use the form below to lodge a formal complaint or appeal.

What can be lodged?

  • Misrepresentation: Of organic status or use of Go4Agri logos.
  • Staff Conduct: Concerns regarding the behavior or professionalism of Go4Agri personnel.
  • Certification Decisions: Appeals against specific decisions made by the certification department.

Note: For certification appeals, an Appeals Committee consisting of three persons will be appointed. No person involved in the original decision will be part of this committee.

Max 5MB. A photo or document is required to follow up on this concern.
