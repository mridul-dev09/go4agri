EU Organic Certification (Regulation (EU) 2018/848)

EU Organic Certification

(Under EU Recognition Process)

Accredited Certification Services

GO4AGRI Certification Pvt. Ltd. is accredited by the International Organic Accreditation Service (IOAS) under ISO/IEC 17065 for certification activities in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2018/848 on organic production and labelling of organic products and its relevant delegated and implementing acts.

GO4AGRI is currently under recognition by the European Union for the purpose of offering certification services under this regulation.

Applicable Standard

Regulation (EU) 2018/848 on organic production and labelling of organic products and relevant delegated and implementing acts.

Accreditation Details

Accreditation Body: IOAS

Standard: ISO/IEC 17065

Contract Number: 310

Geographical Scope: India

Scope of Certification

GO4AGRI is accredited under the following categories:

  • D: Processed agricultural products, including aquaculture products, for use as food
  • E: Feed

Geographical Scope: India

Certification Approach

GO4AGRI conducts certification through a structured process including application review, evaluation of documentation, on-site inspection, certification decision by competent personnel, and surveillance and re-certification.

All certification activities are carried out in accordance with ISO/IEC 17065 principles, ensuring impartiality, consistency, and credibility.

Regulatory Status

Certification services under Regulation (EU) 2018/848 will be offered subject to formal recognition by the European Commission.
